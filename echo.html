Top Five Best Words & Their Definitions

  1. Lagoon: A stretch of salt water separated from the sea by a low sandbank or coral reef.
  2. Surreptitious: Kept secret, especially because it would not be approved of.
  3. Wherewithal: The money or other means needed for a particular purpose.
  4. Zenith: The time at which something is most powerful or successful.
  5. Denouement: The final part of a play, movie, or narrative in which the strands of the plot are drawn together and matters are explained or resolved.
